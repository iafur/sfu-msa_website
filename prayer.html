Prayer Info

Prayer Spaces at SFU Campuses


Prayer spaces are available at all SFU campuses courtesy of the InterFaith Centre:

  • Burnaby (Burnaby Mountain): AQ 3200 (on the east concourse of the AQ between Renaissance Coffee and the Mackenzie cafeteria)
  • Vancouver (Harbour Center): Room 7021*
  • Surrey (Central City): Interfaith 541*
*Please tally on the calendar posted at the Vancouver and Surrey campus prayer areas to show that the prayer space is being used my Muslims.

Friday Prayers (Jumuah Salah)

Friday prayers will be held at 1 PM in BLU 10921 until March 10.
After Daylight Savings Time begins, we will have Friday prayers at 2 PM in EDUC Gym.
There will be samosas for sale after the prayer for $1 each ($1.25 for credit card payments).
